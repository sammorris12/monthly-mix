About

What is Monthly Mix? It’s a collection of playlists of the latest music from each month, with a new playlist going up at the end of every month.

What kind of music will be featured? Anything. Most of it will be indie/underground music though.

I make music/I think you should feature someone. How can I contribute? Feel free to send me an email with a link to a Soundcloud track and I’ll give it a listen. I’ll also promise to listen to every track sent in. Like a shitty John Peel.

How can I keep up with new posts? Keep coming back to this site or subscribe to our newsletter.

What should I do if I have problems with the site? Contact me. This is still fairly new, so jank might appear.

Monthly Mix is a project by @SamMorrisDesign.
